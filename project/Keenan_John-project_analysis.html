Site Intent

Exotic Animal Experience is a small mom and pop type business that delivers an experience of a lifetime! They have several lemurs, Kangaroo Joeys and a Muntjac Deer to experience up in person at this unique petting zoo. They only have one problem, and it is the way they are using their website to bring in new customers.

Currently, the site requires all potential visitors to call the owner about prices and available dates. This could be a hassle for their visitors who may have busy lives and would overall prefer to make reservations online. It also requires users to visit their facebook page to get ticket prices, which is a bit of a problem for older people without facebook profiles (required to sign in).

In order to fix the two following issues above, the redesign would have to include all ticket prices and an easy to use reservation system that should be both easy for the client to access and customer to use. Of course, this would be slightly changing the way Exotic Animal Experience does its work, so business acceptance of this idea is a must.
